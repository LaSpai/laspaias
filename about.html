About

Lazaros Spaias

Lazaros Spaias is a photographer from Thessaloniki, Greece, based in the Netherlands for the past twelve years. His work focuses on street/documentary photography.

Finalist at the Dublin Street Photography Festival 2026 and the Athens Street Photography Festival 2025. Winner of the People's Prize at the IREST Photo Competition at the Sorbonne Art Gallery. His zine Deca||Athens was exhibited in Utrecht. Photographs from his ongoing hardcore punk documentary project have been published in scene fanzines across Europe.
