About

Lazaros Spaias

Lazaros Spaias is a photographer from Thessaloniki, Greece, based in the Netherlands for the past twelve years. His work focuses on street/documentary photography, drawn to subcultures, communities, and the margins of everyday life. Street life in Amsterdam, Utrecht and Athens runs through much of his work, with an eye for the different, the non-mainstream, and the often overlooked.

At shows he works openly, flash in hand, in the middle of the crowd. Not focused on the bands, but on the people: the interactions, the intimacy, the camaraderie. Subcultures change fast. Photography feels like a way to make a record of our time. Something that might matter in fifty years.

  • Finalist, Athens Street Photography Festival (2025)
  • Interview on photography and subcultures published in Desperate Measure fanzine, Vol. 3 (2025)
  • Photographs from his ongoing hardcore punk documentary project published in scene fanzines across Europe
